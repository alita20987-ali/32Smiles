Patient Education

Learn how to maintain a healthy smile and prevent dental problems.

Why Oral Health Matters

A healthy mouth contributes to your overall well-being.

Good oral hygiene prevents cavities, gum disease, and bad breath.

Poor oral health can lead to serious conditions such as heart disease and diabetes. Maintaining your smile is a lifetime commitment.

  • Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste
  • Floss regularly
  • Visit your dentist every 6 months
  • Limit sugary foods and drinks

Common Dental Problems

Tooth Decay

Caused by plaque buildup leading to cavities and pain.

Gum Disease

Infection of gums causing swelling, bleeding, and tooth loss.

Tooth Sensitivity

Pain when consuming hot, cold, or sweet foods.

Healthy Diet

Avoid sugar and drink plenty of water.

Regular Checkups

Early detection prevents serious complications.

Use Mouthwash

Reduces bacteria and keeps breath fresh.

Replace Toothbrush

Change your toothbrush every 3 months.
